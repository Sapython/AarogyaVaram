The Common Cold: An Overview

The common cold is a prevalent viral infection that affects millions of people worldwide. It typically presents with symptoms such as a runny or stuffy nose, sneezing, coughing, and a mild sore throat.

Recognizing Cold Symptoms

Identifying the early signs of a cold can help you take prompt action. Keep an eye out for nasal congestion, watery eyes, fatigue, and a low-grade fever. These symptoms may start gradually and worsen over a few days.
